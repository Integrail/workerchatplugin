How it works

Once initialized, you'll see a floating button in the bottom-right corner of the page. Click it to open the chat interface where you can:

  • Type messages to interact with your AI agent
  • Use voice input for hands-free communication
  • See real-time transcriptions of your speech
  • Receive both text and voice responses
